📖 Research Signal Framework

Derived from "Bridging the Gap from Swiping to IRL" — Berg (2025)

🟢 Authenticity Signals
  • Personal, specific stories
  • Humor consistent with profile
  • Natural vulnerability
  • Reciprocal curiosity
  • Forward momentum
🔴 Concern Signals
  • Telling people what they want to hear
  • No conversational reciprocity
  • Generic, cookie-cutter responses
  • Profile-conversation mismatch
  • Hidden agenda patterns
🟡 Readiness Cues
  • Future-oriented language
  • Logistics surfacing naturally
  • Explicit meeting interest
  • Sustained engagement depth
62% of study participants cited "quality of conversation" as the #1 factor in deciding to meet in person.
